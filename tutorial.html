1hat VHR Testing Flow

A step-by-step guide to testing the Voice-to-Text Recording system

1
Login to the System

Use the credentials that have been shared with you to log into the system.

Username: [Shared Username]
Password: [Shared Password]

2
Select Test User

After logging in, you will see a list of available test users. Currently, there is only one test user available.

Click on the test user to proceed.

3
Start Recording

Once you've selected the test user, you'll be taken to the recording interface.

Click the "Start Recording" button to begin capturing audio.

Make sure your microphone is properly connected and working before starting the recording.

4
Stop Recording

Once you've finished speaking or completed your test script, click the "Stop Recording" button.

5
Submit Recording

After stopping the recording, review it if necessary, then click the "Submit Recording" button to send it for processing.

6
Wait for Processing

The system needs approximately 15-20 seconds to process your recording.

After waiting, refresh the page to see the newly generated record.

If you don't see your recording after refreshing, wait a few more seconds and try refreshing again.

7
Select Record to View

After refreshing, you should see your new recording in the list of available records.

Click on the record you want to view.

8
View and Verify the Record

Review the transcription to check if the recording was correctly transcribed.

Look for any mistakes or inaccuracies in the transcription.

9
Download PDF (Optional)

If needed, you can download a PDF version of the transcription by clicking the "Download PDF" button.

Open the downloaded PDF to verify its contents.
